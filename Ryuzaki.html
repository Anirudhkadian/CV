Ryuzaki (Actual name : Lewliet) aka L

L, is the antagonist in the popular anime series Death Note. L, or as he is named in the show, Ryuzaki was born in England as an orphan and was raised in a church by a man named Watari. He was exceptionally skilled at solving cases, which the police failed to solve. His IQ and observation level much more than average humans. He was a fan of sweets and a Junior Champion in Tennis. He lost his life trying to solve the Kira case, and was succeded by Near and Mello.

Organisations :

  • FBI : Worked Closely with the FBI to solve their cases, even imported
  • Japanese Police : Worked for them on the Kira case, lost his life on the Japanese soil, while working for the same
  • NPA : The task force formed for the Kira Case. L was the head of the team and was later succeded by Light Yagami.

Interests :

  1. Solving Cases
  2. Consuming sweet food items
  3. Playing Tennis
